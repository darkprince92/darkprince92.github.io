I completed my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering at BUET (Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology), one of the most competitive and prestigious engineering universities in Bangladesh. I graduated in September 2015 after a five-year program.

My undergraduate thesis focused on improving evolutionary algorithms for dynamic multi-objective optimization problems. Specifically, I developed heuristic approaches for NSGA-II (a well-known multi-objective genetic algorithm) and proposed a novel random immigrants injection method to improve algorithm performance in dynamic environments where the problem landscape shifts over time.

During my time at BUET, I was recognized for academic and project work: I received a top-10 distinguished poster award at the undergraduate thesis presentation, placed first in a database project showcase organized by the CSE department, and had a project recognized at the Water Hackathon App Fest Bangladesh 2014 (organized by the World Bank).

Relevant coursework: Algorithms, Data Structures, Operating Systems, Database Systems, Software Engineering, Computer Networks, Distributed Systems.
