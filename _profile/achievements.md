## Top 10 Distinguished Poster Award (2015)

Recognized as one of the top 10 posters at the undergraduate thesis poster presentation organized by the Computer Science and Engineering Department at BUET. The presentation was for my thesis on improving NSGA-II with a random immigrants injection method for dynamic multi-objective optimization.

## Water Hackathon App Fest Bangladesh (2014)

One of the top projects recognized at the Water Hackathon App Fest Bangladesh 2014, organized by the World Bank. The hackathon focused on technology solutions for water access and management challenges in Bangladesh.

## 1st Place — Database Project Showcase (2013)

Won first place in the Database Project Showcase organized by the BUET Computer Science and Engineering Department.
