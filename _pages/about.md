I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the [University of Texas at Arlington](https://www.uta.edu/), where I research live programming and interactive feedback for finite model finders. My work focuses on improving accessibility and adoption of formal methods tools for novice users.

My research interests include **Interactive & Live Programming**, **Model-Driven Engineering**, **Formal Methods**, and **Usability of Model Finders**.

Before my PhD, I worked as a software engineer for 7+ years, building large-scale distributed systems and backend services at [Agoda](https://www.agoda.com) (Bangkok), Priyo Sys Ltd. (Dhaka), and iPay Systems Ltd. (Dhaka). I have hands-on experience with AWS, Kafka, Elasticsearch, PostgreSQL, Python, Java, and microservices architecture.
